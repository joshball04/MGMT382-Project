Khaana - Our Story

Bringing Quality Foodservice to Your Table Since 2018


Our Beginning

Khaana started in 2018 when two college friends, Michael James and Sam Chen, realized there was a gap in the market for healthy, convenient meal solutions. They wanted to create something that would help busy professionals and students eat better without spending hours in the kitchen.

Our Mission

At Khaana, we believe that everyone deserves access to nutritious, delicious meals. We are committed to providing high-quality food products and services that make healthy eating simple and enjoyable.

Company Milestones

  1. 2018 - Khaana founded in West Lafayette, Indiana
  2. 2019 - Expanded to serve 5 college campuses across the Midwest
  3. 2021 - Reached 50,000 satisfied customers
  4. 2023 - Opened state-of-the-art meal preparation facility
  5. 2025 - Now serving over 100,000 customers nationwide

What Makes Us Different

  • Fresh Ingredients: We partner with local farms for the freshest produce
  • Customizable Plans: Choose meals that fit your dietary needs
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and ethical sourcing
  • Convenience: Delivered right to your door
